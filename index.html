Reservation Policy

Reservations are accepted for parties of 5 or more guests only.

A $65 deposit is required to secure a reservation. This deposit will be applied to your final check at the end of your meal.

If your group arrives with fewer than 5 guests, the deposit will not be refunded or applied.

Groups of fewer than 5 guests are seated on a walk-in basis only, subject to availability.
